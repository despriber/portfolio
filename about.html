デジタル職人

Guixian Tian

I'm a creative developer based in Fujian, China. For the past 5+ years, I've been crafting digital experiences that blur the line between art and technology.

My journey began in traditional graphic design, but I quickly fell in love with the interactive nature of the web. Today, I specialize in creating immersive WebGL experiences and performant web applications that leave lasting impressions.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring the streets of Fujian, experimenting with generative art, or enjoying a cup of tea.

Philosophy
哲学

In Japanese aesthetics, there's a concept called "Ma" (間)—the space between. It's not just emptiness; it's a pause that gives meaning to what surrounds it. This principle guides everything I create.

I believe that great digital experiences are not about overwhelming users with features, but about creating moments of delight through thoughtful interactions. Every animation, every transition, every micro-interaction should serve a purpose.

My approach combines technical precision with artistic intuition. I write clean, performant code not because it's required, but because craftsmanship matters. The user may never see the code, but they'll feel the difference.

Above all, I strive to create work that respects both the user's time and attention. In a world of infinite scrolling and attention-grabbing tactics, I choose to build experiences that are meaningful, memorable, and human.
